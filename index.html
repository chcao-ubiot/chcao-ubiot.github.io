Chenhong Cao (曹珍红)

Chenhong Cao is an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). She is a core member of the Ubiquitous Battery-free IoT Laboratory led by Prof. Wei Gong. Her research focuses on edge computing, passive IoT systems, and backscatter communication, with a particular emphasis on system architecture and physical-layer co-design for sustainable ambient computing.

She received her Ph.D. from Zhejiang University in 2018. Her work has been recognized by the Xiaomi Young Talents Program and the Shanghai Sailing Program. She has published numerous papers in top-tier venues including IEEE/ACM ToN, IEEE TMC, and IEEE INFOCOM.
