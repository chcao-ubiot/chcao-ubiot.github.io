About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor (with tenure-track) at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am affiliated with the Ubiquitous Battery-free IoT Laboratory led by Prof. Wei Gong.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Wei Dong and Academician Chen Chun. I hold B.S. and M.S. degrees from Northeastern University, China. Before joining USTC, I conducted postdoctoral research at Zhejiang University and served as a faculty member at Shanghai University.

Research Interests: My research focuses on Edge Computing, IoT Systems, and Wireless & Mobile Computing, with a particular emphasis on backscatter communication and passive IoT systems. My work aims to construct high-efficiency, sustainable, and scalable networked intelligence infrastructures for next-generation ambient computing.
