Chenhong Cao

Chenhong Cao is an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China, affiliated with the Ubiquitous Battery-free IoT Laboratory led by Prof. Wei Gong. She received her Ph.D. from Zhejiang University under the supervision of Prof. Wei Dong and Academician Chen Chun.

Her research focuses on backscatter communication, passive IoT systems, edge computing, wireless networking, and mobile computing, with emphasis on protocol/PHY co-design and scalable battery-free sensing. She has served as PI of multiple NSFC projects and contributed to national high-tech programs.

She received the Mozi Distinguished Young Scholar Fellowship (USTC, 2024), Xiaomi Young Talents Program (2024), Shanghai Sailing Program (2020), and Shanghai University Young Talents Program (2022).
