Chenhong Cao

Associate Professor, USTC

I am currently an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am affiliated with the Ubiquitous Battery-free IoT Laboratory led by Prof. Wei Gong.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Zhejiang University in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Wei Dong and Academician Chun Chen. Before joining USTC, I conducted postdoctoral research at Zhejiang University and served on the faculty of Shanghai University.

Research Interests: My research focuses on Edge Computing, IoT Systems and Networking, and Wireless and Mobile Computing. I have a particular emphasis on backscatter communication and passive IoT systems. My work centers on system architecture, protocol/physical-layer co-design, and performance optimization for scalable, battery-free intelligent sensing and connectivity.
